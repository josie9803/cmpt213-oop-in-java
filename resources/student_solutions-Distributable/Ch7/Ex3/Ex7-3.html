Note that each is a subtype of itself by rule 1, but we ignore that possibility.
  1. Object: not a subtype
  2. int: not a subtype
  3. long: not a subtype
  4. int[]: subtype of Object by rule 6, of Cloneable by rule 7
  5. Object[]: subtype of Object by rule 6, of Cloneable by rule 7
  6. int[][]: subtype of Object[] by rule 5, of Object by rule 6, of Cloneable by rule 7
  7. Rectangle: subtype of Cloneable by rule 3, of Object by rule 6
  8. Rectangle[]: subtype of Rectangle2D[] and Object[] by rule 5, of Object by rule 6, of Cloneable by rule 7
  9. Rectangle2D[]: subtype of Object[] by rule 5, of Object by rule 6, of Cloneable by rule 7
  10. Cloneable: subtype of Object by rule 6
