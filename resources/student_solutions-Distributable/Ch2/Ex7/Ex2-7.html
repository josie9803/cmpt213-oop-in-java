Some classes in each category for the car rental context are as follows:
  • Tangible things: car, renter, keys, license tag
  • Agent activities: find car that is available, overdue for return, or due for maintenace
  • Events and transactions: rent, return, purchase, or sell a car
  • Users and roles: renter, sales agent, mechanic, dealer
  • Systems: car rental system, maintenance records subsystem, frequent customer subsystem
  • Systems interfaces: windowing system, customer database, credit card authorization system
  • Foundational classes: Date, String, stack, queue
